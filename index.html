Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article,I would like to give you my
top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan

Best Places to Dine:
  1. Zoom Cafe
  2. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  3. Mango Six
  4. KFC
  5. Elegance
Mango Six. | DanDan, Saipan

Mango Six great drinks which is their forte. They have smoothies, milkteas, and fruit juices. On top of that they also serve foods like fries and desserts.
On top of that they also provide take out and their most popular take out is their sweet garlic fries and pretzels

What I love:

  • Smoothies
  • fries
  • nice looking place
