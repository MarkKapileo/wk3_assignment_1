Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan

Best Places to Dine:
  1. Zoom Cafe
  2. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  3. Mango Six
  4. KFC
  5. 151
Mango Six. | DanDan, Saipan

Mango Six serves great drinks which is their forte. They have smoothies, milkteas, and fruit juices. On top of that they also serve foods like fries and desserts

What I love:

  • Smoothies
  • fries
  • nice looking place
